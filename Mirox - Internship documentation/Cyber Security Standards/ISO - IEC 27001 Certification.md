# CheckList :

### A. **Top-Level ISMS Documents**

1. **ISMS Scope Document** – defines boundaries and applicability.

1. **Information Security Policy** – the company’s top-level commitment.

1. **Objectives and Key Metrics** – measurable goals aligned to policy.

1. **Roles & Responsibilities Matrix (RACI)** – accountability chart.

1. **Statement of Applicability (SoA)** – mandatory! maps Annex A controls to your implementation.

---

### B. **Risk Management Documents**

1. **Asset Inventory** – all information assets, owners, and classifications.

1. **Risk Assessment Methodology** – how you evaluate and score risks.

1. **Risk Assessment Report** – your actual risk register.

1. **Risk Treatment Plan (RTP)** – what’s done for each risk (control applied, accepted, transferred, etc.).

---

### C. **Annex A Control Implementation Evidence**

For **each relevant control (A.5–A.8)**, maintain **policy + procedure + records**:

|Annex A Domain|Example Documents|
|---|---|
|A.5 Organizational Controls|Roles, supplier management policy, segregation of duties|
|A.6 People Controls|NDA, onboarding/offboarding procedures|
|A.7 Physical Controls|Visitor log, access badge policy|
|A.8 Technological Controls|Firewall policy, access review records, patch logs, backup policy|

---

D. **Operational Records**

1. **Incident Management Log**

1. **Access Control Reviews**

1. **Backup and Restore Tests**

1. **Asset Disposal Records**

1. **Change Management Logs**

1. **Vulnerability & Patch Management Reports**

---

### E. **Audit and Review Documents**
